About

I created this Application to help companies manage the work load of daily route delivery drivers. CDL-A holders are limited to 14 hours of work time daily. Sometimes they can go out on a route with over 30 stops and 300 miles of driving and over a thousand cases to deliver.


The mission of this Application is to help routers created a managable route that does not put the driver in jeapordy of running out of hours.
